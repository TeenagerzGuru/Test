About Me

Hi, I'm Apiz from Semenyih, Selangor, currently pursuing a Bachelor's in Game Technology. My ultimate dream is to build a career in Game Security, where I can combine my passion for gaming with a strong commitment to protecting digital worlds.

Before diving into IT, I spent 6 years as a Wilayah Persekutuan's state cyclist — a journey that shaped my background and ethics. The pandemic pushed me to shift gears, and that’s when I discovered my true calling: securing games from the inside out.

Now, I'm on a mission to become part of the next generation of game security — ensuring fair play, integrity, and protection in the world of gaming.
